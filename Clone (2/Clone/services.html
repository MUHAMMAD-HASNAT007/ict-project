Services

Safari Hostel Routes

Buses run between Safari Hostel and the main campus throughout the day.

  • Morning peak service for classes.
  • Evening service for return to hostel.
  • Special trips during exams (check timetable).

Bedian Hostel Routes

Regular shuttle service is available between Bedian Hostel and campus.

  • Fixed pick-up and drop-off points.
  • Reduced frequency on weekends.

Staff & Teacher Transportation

Some routes and timings are reserved for staff and teachers.

  • Morning pick-up from main city points.
  • Afternoon and evening drop-off services.

Special Event Transportation

Extra buses may be arranged for university events and functions.

  • Convocation and sports events.
  • Seminars and conferences.
  • Bookings managed through the Holiday / Event form.
