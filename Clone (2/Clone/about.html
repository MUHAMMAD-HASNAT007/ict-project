About BNU Transport

Transportation Department Overview

The BNU Transport Department manages buses and vans for students, teachers, and staff. It connects hostels, the main campus, and selected city routes.

Mission & Vision

  • Mission: To provide safe, reliable, and affordable transportation.
  • Vision: A modern, technology-based transport system for all BNU users.

Service Areas Covered

  • Safari Hostel ⇔ Campus
  • Bedian Hostel ⇔ Campus
  • City and public routes (selected stops)
  • Staff and teacher transportation
  • Special events and town tours (on request)

Fleet Information

  • Total Buses: 20
  • Mini Vans: 5
  • Special Vehicles: 1 ambulance, 1 maintenance vehicle
  • All vehicles follow regular maintenance schedules.

Service Management

  • Vehicle capacity management and peak hour planning.
  • Backup buses for emergencies and breakdowns.
  • Maintenance logs for each vehicle.
  • Route optimization to save time and fuel.

Safety & Security Overview

  • Verified and trained drivers with proper licenses.
  • Emergency contact numbers displayed inside buses.
  • Basic first-aid kits in vehicles.
  • CCTV monitoring in selected buses.
