Rules & Regulations

Code of Conduct

  • Always carry your university ID or transport pass.
  • Board and exit the bus only at designated stops.
  • Do not damage seats, windows, or other bus property.
  • Keep noise levels low and respect other passengers.

Safety Guidelines

  • Do not stand on bus stairs or near the door while moving.
  • Follow driver and staff instructions at all times.
  • Report unsafe driving or incidents to the transport office.
  • Use emergency contact numbers in case of accidents.

Penalties

  • Fine for damaging property.
  • Temporary suspension from transport services.
  • Permanent ban in serious cases.

Rights & Responsibilities

  • Right to safe and respectful travel environment.
  • Responsibility to follow rules and cooperate with staff.
  • Right to report complaints and give feedback.

Lost & Found

If you lose any item on a bus, report it to the transport office with:

  • Bus route and time.
  • Description of the lost item.
  • Contact details.

Accident / Emergency Protocols

  • Stay calm and follow the driver's instructions.
  • Use emergency exits if required.
  • Call emergency numbers shown inside the bus.
  • Report any injuries to the university health center.
